## Summary


**Summary:**  
System design revolves around building scalable and efficient systems. A core idea is that purchasing additional hardware is often more cost-effective than investing in the optimization of code by developers. [[key|Key]] topics include the use of *master templates* for consistent structure, the importance of load balancers, and comparisons between different approaches to system scalability. Deep dives into research papers and related blogs offer insights into prevalent [[problems|problems]], strategies, and design trade-offs, emphasizing scalability and efficient resource utilization.

## Key Concepts


### Concept


- [[load-balancer|Load Balancer]]: A system component that distributes network or application traffic across multiple servers.

- [[scalable-systems|scalable systems]]: Systems designed to handle increasing amounts of work or to accommodate growth efficiently.
